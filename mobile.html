Performance

Gopher sauce optimizes reading your app's resources by directly using your mobile device's memory as its filesystem. Our SDK makes linking HTML pages to your device's core cpu and task management features seamless. Gopher Sauce's mobile JS layer keeps your mobile devices features such as biometrics, cameras, location and bluetooth inches away.

Bootstrap

Built to make your time more efficient, Gopher sauce uses HTML templating to put together applications in a matter of time. Gopher sauce delivers a new angle to customize your HTML within your mobile apps, and for the first time you can manipulate your DOM elements through Go's template scripting instead of Heap heavy JS.
